## NFT - Market Place

This is a marketplace crypto app that allows traders to list their nfts for sale and they can alse purchase other nfts from the market place.

### Development stacks

- ethers
- hardhat
- tailwindcss
- react@^17.1.0
- next@^12.0.3

### Decisions

Metamask was used to test the nft-market place app.
